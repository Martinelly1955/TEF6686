(e.g: C:\Users\niq_ro\Documents\Arduino\hardware\Arduino_STM32\STM32F1\libraries)
with WIRE library from https://github.com/stm32duino/Arduino_Core_STM32/tree/master/libraries/Wire
You can see details at https://nicuflorica.blogspot.com/2020/03/functionare-corecta-comunicatie-pe-i2c.html
